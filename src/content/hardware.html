HARDWARE

SUPPORTED DEVICES AND PLATFORMS

The microPlatforms are portable and architecture independent. There are three levels of hardware support:

  • Reference devices: globally available, low-cost development hardware with extensive automated end-to-end testing in our CI labs and additional documentation.
  • Community devices: additional builds and documentation for popular hardware platforms, with best-effort support
  • Upstream-supported devices: We provide documentation for using the microPlatforms on any hardware with upstream support

Foundries.io is working with SoC and MCU manufacturers to create a "microPlatform Certified" program.
